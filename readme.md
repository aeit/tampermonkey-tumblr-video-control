## Why?

- Tumblr's autoplay is chaos — this tames it.
- Works after infinite scroll and blog switches.
- Minimal: No buttons, no clutter.
- Future-proof: Handles redesigns.

## Features

- **P**: Play video with sound
- **O**: Play muted
- **M**: Toggle mute on/off
- **Blocks autoplay on videos only** — GIFs keep looping
- **Works after page changes, scroll, and redesigns**

> **GIFs are untouched** — they loop as intended.  
> Targets **only `<video>` elements** with autoplay.  
> Zero hacks, zero CPU waste, zero broken posts.

2. [Get it on GreasyFork](https://greasyfork.org/uk/scripts/554155-tampermonkey-tumblr-video-control)
3. Reload Tumblr — keys work instantly!
